Exercise 1: Let `a = [2, 3]` and `b = [-1, 4]`. Compute `a + b` by hand.
 solution: $$
\begin{aligned}
(x^1 + x^2, y^1 + y^2) &= (2 + (-1), 3 + 4) \\
                       &= (1, 7)
\end{aligned}
$$
